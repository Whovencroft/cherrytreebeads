The story

Rooted in Swannanoa, built through jewelry-making.

Cherry Tree Beads describes itself as a small bead store just outside Asheville, with a focus on personal service, strong product quality, and an inspiring experience whether customers shop online or in the showroom.

The live story also highlights Jennifer Fulton’s long relationship with jewelry-making, her background in marketing and bead retail, and her sourcing trips to China to find new materials and design ideas.

2002Bill Edwards founds Cherry Tree Beads in Evinston, Florida.
2008The website launches and trunk shows expand the business.
2012Jennifer Fulton and Mitchell Baynard purchase the business.
2018The team moves into the restored Swannanoa building used today.
