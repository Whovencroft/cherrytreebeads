About Cherry Tree Beads

A bead store built on product knowledge, personal service, and a real sense of place.

Cherry Tree Beads is a small shop in Swannanoa, North Carolina with a long history, a restored showroom, a strong design point of view, and a commitment to helping customers find the right supplies.

Read the full story Contact Cherry Tree
Founded in 2002

Bill Edwards starts Cherry Tree Beads in Evinston, Florida, first selling on eBay and through trade shows.

Website launched in 2008

Jennifer Fulton joins the story and Cherry Tree begins growing online while also offering trunk shows.

Ownership changes in 2012

Jennifer Fulton and Mitchell Baynard purchase the business and continue expanding it from Western North Carolina.

Swannanoa building in 2018

The team moves into the renovated historic building that now houses the showroom and workspace.
