About Cherry Tree

A small bead store with a long history and a strong point of view.

Cherry Tree Beads began in 2002, launched its website in 2008, changed ownership in 2012, and moved into its restored Swannanoa building in 2018.

Read the storyLive about page

History

Cherry Tree timeline

2002Cherry Tree Beads is founded by Bill Edwards in Evinston, Florida.
2008The website launches and trunk shows expand the business beyond its early eBay roots.
2012Jennifer Fulton and Mitchell Baynard purchase the business.
2018The team moves into the renovated Swannanoa building that now serves as showroom and workspace.

Approach

Personal service and design-led sourcing

Cherry Tree combines online catalog depth with a local showroom, a project-friendly design section, and a more personal retail voice than a generic storefront.
