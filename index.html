About Cherry Tree

A small bead store with a long history and a strong point of view.

Cherry Tree Beads began in 2002, launched its website in 2008, changed ownership in 2012, and moved into its restored Swannanoa building in 2018. That history is part of what makes the store feel personal, established, and rooted in place.

Read the story About Us
2002

Cherry Tree Beads is founded by Bill Edwards in Evinston, Florida.

2008

The website launches and trunk shows expand the business beyond its early eBay roots.

2012

Jennifer Fulton and Mitchell Baynard purchase the business and continue building the brand.

2018

The team moves into the renovated Swannanoa building that now serves as showroom and workspace.
