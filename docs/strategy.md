# Redesign Strategy

## Core shift

The current Cherry Tree Beads homepage has strong inventory breadth and strong brand proof, but the first impression feels overloaded. The redesign direction is:

1. lead with a premium hero and a shorter value statement
2. reduce the number of top-level decisions visible at once
3. feature the best conversion paths first
4. turn design ideas and tutorials into a stronger sales layer
5. surface wholesale and showroom value much earlier

## Homepage priorities

### 1. Stronger first screen
The prototype moves the homepage away from a dense category dump and toward:

- clear headline
- two main calls to action
- visible trust points
- high-quality product photography

### 2. Better retail structure
The first pathways should be:

- New Products
- Gemstone Beads & Pendants
- Design Ideas
- Artisan Made
- Wholesale
- Showroom

This gives both retail and business shoppers a faster route to what matters.

### 3. Better inspiration-to-purchase flow
Cherry Tree has real tutorial and project value, but it should be merchandised more like a dedicated discovery channel. The redesign should connect:

- tutorial pages
- design idea pages
- curated kits
- featured material bundles
- finished-example imagery

### 4. More premium presentation
The current site signals quality in copy, but not enough in hierarchy. The updated design direction uses:

- larger headlines
- more white space
- fewer simultaneous navigation choices
- more editorial product spotlights
- clearer card-based sections
- stronger visual contrast between shopping zones

## Why this should sell better

- fewer first-screen choices usually means less cognitive friction
- stronger photography and curated collections improve perceived quality
- inspiration content closer to shopping paths supports add-on purchases
- wholesale and showroom placement increase trust and business value visibility
- better structure makes repeat visits easier for returning shoppers
