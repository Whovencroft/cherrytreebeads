Wholesale

Wholesale pricing for qualifying designers and bead stores.

Cherry Tree’s wholesale offering is a real competitive advantage. This page keeps the qualification rules, account benefits, and bead-store pricing notes together instead of repeating them in multiple spots.

Open live wholesale infoEmail order@cherrytreebeads.com
Qualification

Send tax ID or EIN documentation

Cherry Tree asks qualifying customers to email a copy of their state tax ID license or EIN documentation before the account is updated.

Account benefit

Coupon codes still work

Wholesale accounts can use coupon codes, which is a useful advantage to keep visible.

Shipping

Free shipping over $48

Qualifying wholesale accounts still receive the same free U.S. shipping threshold on eligible orders.

Ordering

No minimum order requirement

The live wholesale page states there is no minimum order requirement for wholesale orders.

How to apply

Keep the process simple and visible.

  1. Create or use your Cherry Tree account.
  2. Email your state tax ID license or EIN documentation to order@cherrytreebeads.com.
  3. Wait for confirmation before placing the order so discounts and tax exemption can be applied correctly.

For bead stores

Special bead store pricing

Cherry Tree also offers a separate pricing tier for qualifying brick-and-mortar bead stores whose stock is primarily beads, findings, tools, and loose beads.
