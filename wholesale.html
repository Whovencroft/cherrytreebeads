Who qualifies

Stores and designers with tax ID or EIN documentation

Email a copy of the state tax ID license or EIN document to order@cherrytreebeads.com so the account can be updated for wholesale pricing.

Account benefits

Coupon codes, free shipping over $48, and no minimums

These benefits help bead stores, designers, and studios shop with more confidence and flexibility.
